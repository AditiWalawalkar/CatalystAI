# CatalystAI
## AI Co-Scientist for Sustainable Fuel Discovery

> Transforming catalyst discovery from slow trial-and-error experimentation into intelligent AI-driven scientific innovation.

---

# Overview

CatalystAI is an advanced AI-powered catalyst discovery platform designed to accelerate research in sustainable chemistry and clean energy technologies. The platform combines Generative AI, Machine Learning, Explainable AI (XAI), uncertainty-aware prediction systems, and closed-loop feedback learning to enable intelligent scientific discovery.

Traditional catalyst development requires years of experimentation, high computational costs, and hundreds of failed laboratory iterations before identifying efficient catalyst structures. Existing scientific databases are limited because they only retrieve already-known materials and cannot generate entirely new catalyst combinations.

CatalystAI addresses these challenges by functioning as an AI Co-Scientist capable of generating novel catalysts, predicting catalytic performance, explaining scientific reasoning behind predictions, and continuously improving through experimental feedback learning.

---

# Problem Statement

Catalyst discovery remains one of the biggest bottlenecks in sustainable chemistry and industrial research.

### Current Challenges
- Slow trial-and-error experimentation
